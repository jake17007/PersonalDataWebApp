Getting Started With Connectrix

A guide to using the Connectrix platform

Introduction

What is Connectrix?

Connectrix is a platform for secure personal data analytics. We aggregate your personal data from sources like Fitbit, Moves, or YouTube and run it through analytics apps for you to gain insight. Since this takes place on our servers, Connectrix app developers never see your data, but you can still benefit from a wide variety of analytics apps.

How Do I Use Apps?

First you need to establish Connections to data sources. See "Establishing Connections" below for details. Then browse the App Store for analytics apps. See "Using Apps" below for details

Getting Started - Step by Step

Establishing Connections

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard.
  2. Click the "All Connections" button.
  3. Click the "Connect" button next to the data source you'd like to connect.
  4. Follow the provider's instructions. Agree to let Connectrix access your data.
  5. If everything's successful, you'll be redirected to the All Connections page, and you're done!
  6. Now you can start using apps that utilize the data.

Using Apps

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard, and click the "More Apps" button. Or click "Apps Store" in the navigation bar at the top of any page.
  2. Look for apps that uitilize the data source Connections that you want to analyze. These are listed in bold under the description of the app or on the app details page.
  3. Click the app you'd like to use to view the app details.
  4. Make sure you have established all Connections listed under "Connection Requirement."
  5. Click the "Use App" button to add it to your Favorite Apps.
  6. After clicking "Use App," you'll be redirected to you Dashboard.
  7. Click on the app under "My Favorite Apps" to use it!

Creating Apps

To develop your own apps, click "Developers" in the navigation bar at the top of any page. Or click here for the Developer Guide.
